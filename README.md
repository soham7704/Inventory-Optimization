## ğŸ¢ Business Problem Statement

Organizations operating in distributed or hyperlocal supply chains face the following challenges:

1. Frequent stockouts of high-demand SKUs, leading to lost sales and poor customer experience  
2. Excess inventory of slow-moving items, increasing holding and obsolescence costs  
3. Lack of demand predictability, resulting in inefficient replenishment cycles  
4. Absence of a unified KPI framework to monitor inventory health and revenue risk  

This project addresses these challenges by transforming raw operational data into **decision-ready insights** through structured KPIs and demand analysis.
